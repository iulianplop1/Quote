# Quote (Daily Movie Quotes App)
[Live Demo](https://iulianplop1.github.io/Quote/)

## Why I Built This
I built Quote because I am a huge cinephile, but I often found that the most powerful moments from the films I love were lost in the thousands of pages of scripts I've collected. I wanted a way to distill that cinematic wisdom into a daily routine—something that could intelligently find the "gems" in a script and present them to me when I need inspiration the most.

The app uses AI to do the heavy lifting, parsing through dense scripts to find quotes with high narrative significance. By automating this, I created a platform where I can manage my entire script library and rediscover iconic lines from movies like *The Dark Knight* or *Inception* in a beautiful, minimalistic interface. It's about bringing the magic of the big screen into a small, daily digital experience.

## Features
- **AI Script Analysis**: Uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to intelligently parse scripts and extract quotes based on their narrative significance.
- **Morning Routine Integration**: Create scheduled routines that play your favorite quotes alongside a background song to start your day.
- **Thematic Discovery**: Search your script library by abstract themes like "justice," "fear," or "hope" to find relevant quotes instantly.
- **Cinematic Context**: View the exact scene surrounding any quote to understand the character's motivation and the plot context.
- **Secure Backend**: Uses Supabase for secure data storage and authentication, ensuring your personal library is private and safe.
